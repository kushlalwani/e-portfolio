Writing Sample

Technical Definition and Description

This document explains the Drag Reduction System (DRS), a key aerodynamic feature used in Formula 1 racing to improve overtaking opportunities. It introduces foundational concepts such as drag, downforce, and dirty air, then builds into a structured explanation of how the system operates.

The document also breaks down the mechanical components of DRS and outlines the sequence of events required for activation during a race, connecting technical theory to real-world application.

Reflection

This piece demonstrates my ability to take a complex technical system and explain it in a structured and accessible way. One of the main challenges was balancing technical accuracy with clarity, especially when introducing aerodynamic concepts to a general audience. I learned how to define key terms, organize information logically, and use visuals to support understanding. This project reflects my strength in technical communication and my ability to break down complex systems into clear, step-by-step explanations for non-expert readers.
